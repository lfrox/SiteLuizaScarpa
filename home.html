About

Luiza Scarpa is a visual artist, she started painting from a need to express what she cannot find in words. The works are the result of reflections with many layers and textures of the most varied, vibrant colors or the absence of them. The screens reflect trials, hits and misses, just as life is. The materials used to shape the works are composed of resin, acrylic paint, spackle, silicone, wax and sustainable materials. The choice of materials used is an attempt to reflect on the viewer universal emotions within a singularity.
